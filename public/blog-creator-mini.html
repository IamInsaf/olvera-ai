Create SEO-Optimized Blog Post

Include your main keyword in the title (ideally at the beginning). Keep it under 60 characters.
Summarize your content in 150-160 characters. Include your main keyword naturally.
Use a high-quality image with descriptive filename (e.g., career-guidance-after-12th.jpg).

SEO Tips:

  • Use headings (H2, H3) to structure your content
  • Include your keyword in the first paragraph
  • Add internal links to related content
  • Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
Use lowercase with hyphens between words. Keep it short and descriptive.
