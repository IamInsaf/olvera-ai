AI in Career Counselling – Is it Better Than Human Mentors?

By Muhammed Insaf • 5/5/2025

Introduction

Career counselling is no longer confined to face-to-face meetings with school counsellors or educational consultants. With the rise of Artificial Intelligence (AI), the world of career guidance is being reshaped in unimaginable ways.

Today, many students are asking:

“Can AI really help me choose my career?”

“Is AI better than human mentors?”

In this article, we’ll explore how AI is revolutionizing career counselling, compare it with traditional methods, and help you decide what’s best for your future.

What Is AI Career Counselling?

AI career counselling uses algorithms, data analysis, and machine learning to:


  • Analyze your interests and aptitude
  • Match you with career options
  • Suggest personalized learning paths
  • Track your progress over time

Platforms like Olvera.in use AI to provide career suggestions through quizzes, skill assessments, and personalized reports.

How AI Works in Career Guidance

Here’s a step-by-step example of how AI helps:


  1. Collects Data
  • Through questionnaires, tests, and student input.
  • Includes personality, interests, goals, skills, academic performance.
  1. Analyzes Patterns
  • Compares user data with success patterns from lakhs of previous students.
  • Matches you to career paths where similar profiles have excelled.
  1. Generates Recommendations
  • Suggests top 3–5 careers.
  • Provides roadmap: courses to take, entrance exams to prepare, skills to build.

  • As more students use the system, the AI becomes smarter.

Benefits of AI Career Counselling

✅ 1. Personalized Suggestions at Scale

Unlike generic advice, AI evaluates your exact profile and offers tailored guidance — even if 10,000 students use it in a day.


✅ 2. Bias-Free Decisions

AI doesn’t push engineering or medicine just because they’re “popular.” It evaluates your fit based on logic and data.


✅ 3. Instant Feedback

You don’t have to wait for appointments. Career suggestions and roadmaps are available instantly.


✅ 4. Cost-Effective

Most AI-based tools are affordable (or free) compared to hiring professional counsellors.


✅ 5. Available 24x7

Whether it’s 3 AM or a weekend, AI-based platforms are always accessible.


✅ 6. Data-Driven Accuracy

AI considers market trends, college outcomes, and job scope — all backed by real data.

Limitations of AI Counselling

❌ 1. Lack of Human Emotion

AI cannot understand emotional stress, family pressure, or anxiety like a human mentor can.


❌ 2. Dependent on Inputs

Garbage in, garbage out. If you give dishonest or incomplete answers, the guidance won’t help.


❌ 3. Generic Soft Skills Advice

While AI can suggest career paths, it may not provide in-depth soft skills training or motivational support.


❌ 4. Less Scope for Follow-up

AI tools may not track your progress unless you consistently return to the platform.

Benefits of Human Mentors

✅ 1. Empathy and Understanding

Human counsellors can support students emotionally, help with decision conflicts, and encourage confidence.


✅ 2. Contextual Advice

They can understand family background, budget, societal pressures, and suggest practical alternatives.


✅ 3. Mentorship

Human mentors can act as long-term career guides, offering personalized insights, networking, and encouragement.

Limitations of Human Counsellors

❌ 1. Limited Reach

A single counsellor can guide maybe 10–20 students in a day. AI can handle thousands.


❌ 2. Inconsistency in Advice

Different counsellors might give conflicting suggestions for the same student profile.


❌ 3. Bias

Some mentors may unknowingly push traditional fields like medicine/engineering and discourage unconventional careers.


❌ 4. Expensive

Quality career mentors can charge thousands of rupees per session.

AI + Human Mentor = Best Combo

  • Use human mentors for emotional guidance, confidence building, and deeper context.
