Top 10 Courses After 12th Commerce (2025 Edition)

1. Chartered Accountancy (CA)

  • Duration: 4-5 years
  • Scope: ₹6-25 LPA (Fresh CAs earn ₹6-8 LPA)
  • Best For: Students strong in accounting and taxation

2. Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Specializations:
  • Accounting & Finance
  • Banking & Insurance
  • E-Commerce

3. Company Secretary (CS)

  • Growing Demand: 18% increase in corporate compliance jobs
  • Salary Range: ₹5-18 LPA

4. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

  • Career Paths:
  • Management roles
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Corporate leadership

5. Economics Honors

  • New Opportunities:
  • Economic analysts in tech companies
  • Policy researchers for NGOs

6. Digital Marketing Certification

  • High Growth: 40% annual increase in digital marketing jobs
  • Starting Salary: ₹3-6 LPA

7. Financial Technology (FinTech) Courses

  • Future Scope:

8. Bachelor of Law (LLB)

  • Emerging Areas:

  • Intellectual property rights

9. Data Science for Commerce Students
