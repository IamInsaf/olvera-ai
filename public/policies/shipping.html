Olvera Shipping Policy

At Olvera, we specialize in delivering career guidance and talent discovery services through digital platforms. Please review our shipping policy to understand how and when your services will be delivered.

🚚 No Physical Shipping

We do not ship any physical products. All our services are delivered digitally via the Olvera platform, your dashboard, or email (e.g., certificates, test results).

⚡ Instant Delivery

Most services, such as test access, AI results, and digital certificates, are delivered instantly or within a few minutes after successful payment.

📥 Delivery Channels

  • Career test access – via your Olvera dashboard
  • Results & reports – shown on-screen and downloadable
  • Certificates – available on the platform or sent via email

🛠️ Delays & Troubleshooting

In rare cases, if you do not receive access within 15 minutes of payment, please contact our support team. We’ll help you manually verify and activate your services.
